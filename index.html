Welcome to ROAR!
The Roar Cultural Festival is a vibrant celebration of art, culture, and community. Each year, thousands of visitors gather in the heart of Fullerton to enjoy live performances, cultural showcases, delicious food, and unique vendor experiences.

This is your opportunity to connect with the community, share your products and services, and celebrate diversity with us.

Vendor Categories

We welcome a wide range of vendors who represent creativity, culture, and community:

Fine Art Vendors
Original artworks (painting, photography, sculpture)
Commercial Vendors
For-profit businesses offering cultural or lifestyle products
Business & Organization Vendors
Handmade artisan goods
(jewelry, pottery, textiles, candles),
civic and cultural groups
Food Vendors
Authentic food and beverages

Why Join?

  • Showcase your work to a diverse and engaged audience
  • Gain exposure through festival promotions and media outreach
  • Join a celebration of cultural diversity in the heart of Orange County
  • Connect with community leaders, artists, and fellow entrepreneurs
